ABOUT TSRC

Welcome to our Traffic Sign Recognition & Classification (TSRC) platform! We're dedicated to providing innovative solutions for enhancing road safety through cutting-edge technology. Our website offers a range of features aimed at enabling live detection and classification of traffic signs, along with comprehensive educational resources on TSRC technology.

Mission Statement:

Our mission is to leverage the power of machine learning to develop advanced TSR systems that improve driver awareness, facilitate autonomous navigation, and contribute to the development of smarter and safer transportation infrastructure.
